GiantKelpDynamics

$GiantKelpDynamics.jl$ is a Julia package providing a dynamical model for the motion (and in the future the growth and biogeochemical interactions) of giant kelp (Macrocystis pyrifera).

The kinematic model is based on the work of Utter and Denny (1996) and Rosman et al. (2013), and used in Strong-Wright and Taylor (undated).

We have implemented it in the particle in the framework of Strong-Wright et al. (2023) and the coupled with the fluid dynamics of Ramadhan et al. (2020).
